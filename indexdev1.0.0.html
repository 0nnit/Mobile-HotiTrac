HortiTrac

Documenting clean and healthy canabis cultivation habbits in a variety of envorinments; for the advancement of the comunity.

A visual, easy to use solution for quick management and documentation.

or

HortiTrac is a Garden Data Management System (GDMS) designed to ease the stresses of documenting your home or comercial garden. Targeting the fastly growing canabis industry we are looking to provide a one stop shop when it comes to storing and reviewing information on the vast number of plant strains that could end up in your garden.

At HortiTrac we aim to collect and document clean and healthy growing habbits that could one day be used in defining what is the clean cultivation of marijunana during the legislation process or even to aid in the growing of your very own garden.

Imagine having four differnet strains in your garden and having to remember what you introduced into each of the strain's ecosystem that it benifited from and and what it did not benifit from. Now take those four different plants that you have in your small home garden and imaging multiplying them by ten giving you now forty plants to trac in your now small comercial garden.
